The Model Hub — Special Section

The Zenkai Model Hub lets you share trained AI agents with other users. Sharing is ALWAYS your choice.

What gets shared when you upload to the Hub:

  • Model weights (SafeTensors format only — cannot contain executable code)
  • Training configuration (hyperparameters, reward settings, scenario)
  • Metadata you provide (agent name, description, tags)
  • Your chosen display name (NOT your real name or email)
  • Performance stats (reward scores, episode count, training duration)
  • Hardware class (e.g. "mid-range-gpu" — NOT your specific GPU model)

What NEVER gets shared through the Hub:

  • Your email address
  • Your beta/license key
  • Your file system paths or computer name
  • Your real name (unless you choose it as your display name)

Security

  • All uploaded models MUST be in SafeTensors format. This format physically cannot contain executable code — it's pure mathematical data.
  • Metadata is sanitized on upload to strip any system-identifying information.
  • We validate file structure and reject anything that doesn't match expected formats.

Your rights over shared content:

  • You can delete any model you've uploaded at any time
  • You retain full ownership of your trained models (see Terms of Service)
  • Deleting your account removes all your Hub uploads
