3. Screen Capture — Special Section

Screen capture is the most sensitive data processing Zenkai performs. Here's exactly what happens.

What Is Captured

During AI training sessions, Zenkai captures frames from your screen so the AI agent can "see" the game. This captures whatever is visible on your display at the time — the game window, and potentially other visible applications, notifications, or sensitive information.

How Captures Are Processed

  • Locally only. Screen frames are processed in real-time on your machine by the AI model.
  • Frames are used for immediate inference and are not saved as images.
  • No screen capture data is transmitted to our servers. Ever.
  • Training metrics (reward scores, episode counts) are logged locally as numerical data, not images.

Legal Basis

Screen capture requires your explicit consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3), as implemented by Danish Cookiebekendtgørelsen). Consent is obtained:

  • On first launch via the Beta & Screen Capture Disclaimer (one-time acceptance)
  • Each time you click "Start Training" (per-session consent)

You can withdraw consent at any time by stopping the training session. No screen data persists after the session ends.

Your Precautions

Before starting a training session:

  • Close sensitive applications (banking, messaging, email)
  • Disable desktop notifications
  • Ensure only the target game is visible

4. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Screen captures — never leave your machine, never transmitted
  • Mouse/keyboard inputs during training — processed locally, not logged
  • Your trained AI models — stored locally unless YOU choose to upload
  • Your training configurations — never leave your machine
  • Your hardware specs — checked locally, never transmitted
  • Your browsing habits — we don't track you
  • Your location — we don't need it

5. Nexus — Shared Content

Sharing is always your choice. When you upload a Spark:

What Gets Shared:

  • Model weights (SafeTensors format — cannot contain executable code)
  • Training configuration (hyperparameters, reward settings, scenario)
  • Metadata you provide (name, description, tags)
  • Your chosen display name (NOT your real name or email)

What NEVER Gets Shared:

  • Your email address, password, or auth tokens
  • Your file system paths or computer name
  • Your Discord account details (beyond display name)

6. Where Your Data Lives

  • Account data, Nexus content: Our server infrastructure within the EU
  • Training data, screen captures, AI models: YOUR machine, locally, in AppData\Zenkai\
  • Discord OAuth data: Processed via Discord's API (Discord Inc., San Francisco, USA)

7. International Data Transfers

If you use Discord OAuth, your Discord user ID and username are processed by Discord Inc. in the United States. This transfer is covered by the EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy decision (European Commission, July 10, 2023).

All other personal data is processed within the EU/EEA.
